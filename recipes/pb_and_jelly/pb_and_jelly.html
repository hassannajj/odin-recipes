Peanut Butter & Jelly

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon jelly (grape, apricot)
  • 1 butterknive

Steps:

  1. Get a plate
  2. Place one tablespoon of jelly on one slice of bread and spread it with the knife
  3. Place one tablespoon of peanut butter on the other slice and spread it
  4. Put the slices together and enjoy your sandwich!
